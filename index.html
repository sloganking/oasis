Oasis

A heart-centered, sex-positive community house in Arizona

Welcome Aboard

Oasis is a five-bedroom community house where open-hearted people live, grow, and explore together. We're building a home that honors both depth and play — a place where you can be fully yourself.

The house carries an ocean and boat theme (courtesy of Captain Lin and our ocean-loving homeowner), but each room is yours to make your own. Bring your vibe, your art, your altar — whatever makes a space feel like home.

Our Values

We're a small crew, not a commune with a rulebook. But these are the currents that run through the house:

Heart-Centered Connection

We lead with openness and authenticity. Real relating over surface-level pleasantries.

Sex-Positivity & Consent

Eros is welcome here. So are clear boundaries, honest communication, and enthusiastic consent.

Personal Sovereignty

You don't have to shrink to belong. We celebrate each person standing in their own truth.

Growth Over Comfort

We welcome honest reflection and feedback as doorways to deeper connection and self-knowledge.

Communal Care

Shared meals, shared spaces, shared presence. We take care of the home and each other.

Playfulness

Life here doesn't have to be heavy. We make room for joy, humor, spontaneity, and adventure.
